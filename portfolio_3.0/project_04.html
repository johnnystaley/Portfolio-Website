Podcast Series

Theory of Mind

My entire life I have always been intrigued when discussing topics about life, philosophy, science, business, and the world at large. Often, I would find myself discussing such topics with friends for hours on end without even noticing the passage of time. The only difference now is that I have decided to grab a camera and a microphone and have started to search the internet for interesting people to have these conversations with.

Theory of Mind is the title of the podcast that I have decided to create. In short, the defintion of theory of mind refers to ones capacity to understand other people by ascribing knowledge to them such as beliefs, intentions, desires, emotions, and thoughts. I can think of no better name than Theory of Mind, for a podcast with a mission to understand the most intriguing ideas of our modern world.

The first episode of Theory of Mind features Dr. Sandra Spataro, a professor at Northern Kentucky University and co-author of Dare to Inspire, a book that focuses on unlocking and sustaining inspiration within yourself.

The second episode of Theory of Mind features Cole Napper, VP of People Analytics at Orgnostic and host of the Directionally Correct Podcast.

If curiosity is something that you value, I guarantee you will enjoy following me on my journey to understand more about our world. Theory of Mind is available now on YouTube, Spotify, and coming soon on Apple Podcasts.
